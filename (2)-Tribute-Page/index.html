The old Saumarez Park

Key Features

  • Spinning Plate (Roundabout) - Was insanely fun to be spun on. When it went too fast you would fall into the woodchips below.
  • The Tiniest Slide - An adventure, but before you know it you're at the bottom.
  • Large Climbing Frame - Spanned most of the park, but would be very busy at peak times as it was genuinely fun.
  • Swings - Very fun, but very few of them.
  • Zip Line - The best part of the park, but the seat always got stolen.
  • Benches Everywhere - For easy access to parents, who you would run to after getting another splinter.
